The ESP32 is a small microcontroller that costs under $8 and connects to your WiFi. With ESPHome you can build your own sensors, switches, and displays - no programming required.

<Aside type="note" title="Open Source">
ESPHome is open source! See [ESPHome on GitHub](https://github.com/esphome/esphome) for documentation and examples.
</Aside>

## 🛒 What do you need?

<Aside type="tip" title="Starter kit (~$15)">
- **ESP32-WROOM-32 DevKit** - Recommended for beginners
- **USB cable** (micro-USB or USB-C)
- **Home Assistant** running on your server
</Aside>

### Recommended boards

| Board | Price | Best for |
|-------|-------|----------|
| ESP32-WROOM-32 DevKit | $5-8 | Beginners |
| ESP32-C3 Super Mini | $4 | Small projects |
| ESP32-S3 | $8 | Displays, camera |
| D1 Mini (ESP8266) | $4 | Simple sensors |

<Aside type="note" title="Where to buy?">
- **Fast:** Amazon or a local electronics retailer (Danish readers: Proshop, av-cables.dk, elektronik-lavpris.dk)
- **Cheap:** AliExpress (2-3 weeks delivery)
</Aside>
