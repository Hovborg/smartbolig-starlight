# Cookie Policy for SmartBolig.net

**Last updated:** July 2026

This cookie policy explains how SmartBolig.net uses cookies and similar technologies.

---

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## Types of cookies we use

### Necessary cookies
These cookies are essential for the website to function correctly, and they cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Expires |
|--------|---------|---------|
| CookieConsent | Remembers your cookie choice | 1 year |

### Analytical cookies
Used to understand how visitors use the website.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Expires |
|--------|----------|---------|---------|
| _ga | Google Analytics | Distinguishes users | 2 years |
| _ga_* | Google Analytics | Session tracking | 2 years |

### Marketing cookies
Used to show relevant ads.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose |
|--------|----------|---------|
| IDE | Google | Ad delivery |
| DSID | Google | Ad personalization |
| ez_* | Ezoic | Ad optimization |

## Managing your cookies

### Via our cookie banner
Click the cookie icon in the bottom-left corner to change your preferences.

### Via your browser
You can delete or block cookies in your browser settings:
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/clear-cookies-and-site-data-firefox)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

## Consent

The first time you visit SmartBolig.net, a cookie banner appears where you can:
- **Accept all:** Allows all cookies
- **Necessary only:** Allows only essential cookies
- **Customize:** Choose which categories to allow

Your consent is stored for 12 months.
