# Legal Information

At SmartBolig.net, we take transparency and data protection seriously. As a Danish website about smart home technology, we are committed to complying with both Danish and European data protection regulations, including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

## Our commitment to you

We believe you as a user have the right to know:

- **What data we collect** - We only use the most necessary cookies to improve your experience on the site
- **How we make money** - We are transparent about our affiliate partnerships and how they affect our recommendations
- **How we protect you** - Your personal data is processed in accordance with GDPR and Danish law
- **What you agree to** - Clear terms of use so you know what applies when using our content

<Aside type="note" title="GDPR Compliance">
SmartBolig.net is based in Denmark and complies with the EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You always have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
</Aside>

## Our content principles

All content on SmartBolig.net is created with a focus on helping you build a smart home. When we recommend products, it's based on our own experiences and research. If we receive a commission for a purchase, it does not affect our assessment of the product - we only recommend products we would use ourselves.

## Your rights under GDPR

As an EU citizen, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Right of access** | You can request a copy of the data we have about you |
| **Right to rectification** | You can ask us to correct inaccurate information |
| **Right to erasure** | You can ask us to delete your data ("the right to be forgotten") |
| **Right to restriction** | You can ask us to restrict the processing of your data |
| **Right to data portability** | You can request your data in a readable format |
| **Right to object** | You can object to our processing of your data |
