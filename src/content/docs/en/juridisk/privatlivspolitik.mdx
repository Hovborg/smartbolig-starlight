# Privacy Policy for SmartBolig.net

**Last updated:** July 2026

At SmartBolig.net we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information.

---

## 1. Data Controller

**SmartBolig.net**  
Denmark  
Email: kontakt@smartbolig.net

---

## 2. What information do we collect?

### 2.1 Information you provide
- Email address (if you sign up for the newsletter)
- Comments or messages you send

### 2.2 Automatically collected information
- IP address (anonymized in Google Analytics; Cloudflare and advertising partners process the full IP address as part of operations and security)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time of visit
- Referring website

---

## 3. Why do we collect data?

We collect data to:
- Improve our website and content
- Analyze traffic and user behavior
- Show relevant ads
- Respond to inquiries

**Legal basis:** Analytical and marketing cookies are only used with your consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). Operating and securing the website, and responding to inquiries, are based on our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)).

---

## 4. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for:
- **Necessary cookies:** Ensure the website works correctly
- **Analytical cookies:** Google Analytics for traffic analysis
- **Marketing cookies:** To show relevant ads via Google AdSense and Ezoic

You can manage your cookie preferences via our cookie banner or on our [cookie policy](/en/juridisk/cookiepolitik/) page.

---

## 5. Data sharing

We only share data with:
- **Google Analytics** - Traffic analysis
- **Google AdSense** - Advertising
- **Ezoic** - Advertising and optimization
- **Cloudflare** - Security and CDN

Processing by these providers is governed by data processing agreements, and transfers to the US rely on the EU-US Data Privacy Framework or the EU's standard contractual clauses.

---

## 6. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:
- **Access:** See what data we hold about you
- **Rectification:** Have incorrect information corrected
- **Erasure:** Have your data deleted
- **Restriction:** Limit the processing of your data
- **Objection:** Object to processing
- **Data portability:** Receive a copy of your data

Contact us at kontakt@smartbolig.net to exercise your rights.

---

## 7. Data security

We protect your data with:
- SSL/TLS encryption
- Secure hosting via Cloudflare
- Regular security updates

---

## 8. Data retention

We keep data for as long as it is necessary for the purpose, or as required by law. Anonymized analytics data in Google Analytics is retained for up to 14 months.

---

## 9. Children's privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced on the website.

## 11. Affiliate links and recommendations

SmartBolig.net participates in affiliate programs, which means we may receive commission when you buy products via links on our site.

### How it works
- We link to products we use or recommend ourselves
- If you buy via our links, we receive a small commission
- **It costs you nothing extra** - the price is the same

### Our affiliate partners
- Proshop.dk
- Specialkamera.dk

The current partner list is always available in our [affiliate disclosure](/en/juridisk/affiliate-disclosure/).

### Our commitment
- We only recommend products we would use ourselves
- Affiliate revenue does **not** influence our recommendations
- We always disclose when a page contains affiliate links
