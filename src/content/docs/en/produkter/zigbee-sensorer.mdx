Zigbee sensors are **the foundation of smart home automations**. They detect motion, temperature, open doors and much more - sending data to Home Assistant via your Zigbee network. With long battery life and lightning-fast response, they're ideal for automations.

<Aside type="tip" title="Top Recommendations 2025">
- **Motion**: Aqara Motion Sensor P1 (~$15)
- **Door/Window**: Aqara Door Sensor (~$12) or Sonoff SNZB-04P (~$10)
- **Temperature**: Sonoff SNZB-02P (~$11) - Best value
- **Presence**: Aqara FP300 (~$50) or Sonoff SNZB-06P (~$22)
- **Water Leak**: Aqara Water Sensor (~$18)
</Aside>

### 🥇 Aqara Motion Sensor P1 - Best Choice

The most versatile PIR sensor with **adjustable detection time from 1 second to 200 seconds**. Perfect for lighting automations where you want immediate response.

**Pros:**
- ✅ Adjustable cooldown (1-200 sec) via Zigbee2MQTT
- ✅ 170° detection angle
- ✅ 5-year battery life
- ✅ Includes 360° adjustable mount
- ✅ Works perfectly with Home Assistant

**Cons:**
- ❌ More expensive than alternatives
- ❌ Marketed for Aqara Hub (but works fine with Z2M/ZHA)

**Buy:** [Amazon](https://amazon.com), [AliExpress](https://aliexpress.com)

### 💰 Budget: Sonoff SNZB-03P

Good budget option with 60-second fixed cooldown. The new P-series has better design and longer battery life.

**Buy:** [ITEAD](https://itead.cc), [AliExpress](https://aliexpress.com) - ~$12

### 🏠 IKEA VALLHORN

Cheapest option from a known brand. Uses rechargeable AAA batteries (IKEA LADDA recommended). IP44 rating = can be used outdoors.

**Buy:** [IKEA](https://ikea.com) - $10

### 🥇 Aqara FP300 - Best Zigbee Presence

The **first battery-powered mmWave sensor** with Zigbee. Perfect for bathrooms and offices.

**Pros:**
- ✅ Battery-powered (can be placed anywhere)

- ✅ 24 adjustable detection distances
- ✅ Zigbee mode with full configuration in Z2M

**Cons:**
- ❌ Requires Zigbee firmware flash via Aqara app

Cheapest mmWave sensor on the market. Simple but effective.

**Pros:**
- ✅ Very cheap

**Cons:**
- ❌ Requires USB power

### 🏆 Premium: Aqara FP2
