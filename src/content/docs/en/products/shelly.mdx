Shelly is a Bulgarian-based company making some of the best and most reliable smart home products on the market. **Everything works 100% locally** without cloud, has fantastic Home Assistant integration, and is designed in the EU.

<Aside type="tip" title="Why Shelly?">
- **Local control** - No cloud required, works even without internet
- **Home Assistant** - Fantastic integration via WiFi/MQTT
- **Scripting** - Advanced automation directly on the device
- **Warranty** - 3 years on Plus/Gen3/Gen4, 5 years on Pro series
- **EU-based** - Designed and developed in Bulgaria
</Aside>

## 🔧 Home Assistant Integration

Shelly has **native integration** in Home Assistant - no MQTT required (but supported).

<Steps>
1. Connect your Shelly device to WiFi via Shelly app
2. Home Assistant finds it automatically via discovery
3. Click "Configure" and add the device
4. All entities are created automatically
</Steps>

### Advanced: MQTT

For more control you can enable MQTT:

1. Go to Shelly's web interface (IP address)
2. Settings → MQTT
3. Enable MQTT and enter your broker
4. Use Mosquitto in Home Assistant
