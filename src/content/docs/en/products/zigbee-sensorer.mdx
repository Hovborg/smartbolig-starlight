Zigbee sensors are **the foundation of smart home automations**. They detect motion, temperature, open doors and much more - sending data to Home Assistant via your Zigbee network. With long battery life and lightning-fast response, they're ideal for automations.

<Aside type="tip" title="Top Recommendations 2025">
- **Motion**: Aqara Motion Sensor P1 (~$15)
- **Door/Window**: Aqara Door Sensor (~$12) or Sonoff SNZB-04P (~$10)
- **Temperature**: Sonoff SNZB-02P (~$11) - Best value
- **Presence**: Aqara FP300 (~$50) or Sonoff SNZB-06P (~$22)
- **Water Leak**: Aqara Water Sensor (~$18)
</Aside>
