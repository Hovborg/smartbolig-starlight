## 4. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for:
- **Necessary cookies:** Ensures the website works correctly
- **Analytical cookies:** Google Analytics for traffic analysis
- **Marketing cookies:** To show relevant ads via Google AdSense and Ezoic

You can manage your cookie preferences via our cookie banner.

## 5. Data sharing

We only share data with:
- **Google Analytics** - Traffic analysis
- **Google AdSense** - Advertising
- **Ezoic** - Advertising and optimization
- **Cloudflare** - Security and CDN

All third parties are GDPR compliant.

## 6. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:
- **Access:** See what data we have about you
- **Rectification:** Have incorrect information corrected
- **Erasure:** Have your data deleted
- **Restriction:** Limit processing of your data
- **Objection:** Object to processing
- **Data portability:** Receive your data

Contact us at kontakt@smartbolig.net to exercise your rights.
