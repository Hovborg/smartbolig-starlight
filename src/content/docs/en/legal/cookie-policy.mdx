# Cookie Policy for SmartBolig.net

**Last updated:** December 2024

This cookie policy explains how SmartBolig.net uses cookies and similar technologies.

---

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## How we use cookies

We use cookies for:

### Necessary cookies
These cookies are required for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| CookieConsent | Remembers your cookie preferences | 1 year |

### Analytical cookies
These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| _ga | Google Analytics - identifies unique users | 2 years |
| _gid | Google Analytics - identifies sessions | 24 hours |

### Marketing cookies
These cookies are used to show relevant ads.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Google AdSense | Personalized ads | Varies |
| Ezoic | Ad optimization | Varies |
