# Affiliate Disclosure

**Last updated:** December 2025

<Aside type="tip" title="In short">
When you buy products through links on SmartBolig.net, we may receive a small commission. It doesn't cost you extra, and it helps us keep the site running.
</Aside>

---

## What are affiliate links?

Affiliate links are special links to products in webshops. When you click the link and buy the product, we receive a small percentage of the sale as commission.

**Important:** The price for you is exactly the same, whether you use our link or not.

---

## Why do we use affiliate links?

SmartBolig.net is a free website. Affiliate revenue helps us to:

- Pay for hosting and domain
- Buy products for testing and reviews
- Spend time writing quality content
- Keep the site ad-light

On pages with affiliate links you will typically see:

- Text like "Affiliate link" or "Ad"
