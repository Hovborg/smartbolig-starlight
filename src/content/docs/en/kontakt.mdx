## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**What is SmartBolig.net?**

SmartBolig.net is a Danish guide to smart home technology. We write guides, reviews and tutorials about everything from Home Assistant to smart products that can make your home more intelligent, secure and energy-efficient. While our content is primarily in Danish, we also offer English translations.

**Are your guides free?**

Yes, all content on SmartBolig.net is freely available. We earn money through affiliate links, but this never affects our recommendations - we only recommend products we would use ourselves.

**How do you choose which products to recommend?**

We only recommend products based on our own experiences and thorough research. We prioritize products that:
- Work with Home Assistant and other open platforms
- Have good quality and durability
- Offer good value for money
- Don't require cloud connection (when possible)

### Technical Help

**Can you help me set up my smart home?**

We don't offer direct support or setup services, but our guides are written so you can get started yourself. If you're stuck, you can describe your problem via email, and we'll try to help or point you to relevant resources.

**My Home Assistant installation isn't working - what do I do?**

First check our troubleshooting tips in the relevant installation guide. If the problem persists, describe your setup (hardware, installation method, error messages) when you contact us. Screenshots are always helpful.

**Which smart home platform do you recommend?**

We recommend Home Assistant for most users. It's free, open source, and gives you full control over your data. Start with our [Getting Started guide](/en/home-assistant/getting-started/).

### About the Website

**How often do you update your content?**

We continuously update our guides when new software versions are released or better methods become available. Major updates are marked with a date in the guide.

**Can I contribute content?**

Yes! If you have experience with smart home technology and want to share your knowledge, you're welcome to contact us about guest posts or collaboration.

**How can I support SmartBolig.net?**

The best way to support us is to use our affiliate links when you buy products. It doesn't cost you extra, but gives us a small commission that helps keep the site running.
